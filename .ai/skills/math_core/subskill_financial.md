# Sub-Skill: Financial Mathematics
**Focus:** Portfolio Theory & Risk Metrics.
**Key Formulas:**
- **Kelly Criterion (Continuous):** $f^* = \mu / \sigma^2$
- **Sortino Ratio:** $R_S = (R_p - R_f) / \sigma_d$ (Downside Deviation).
- **Drawdown Dynamics:** Cálculo recursivo de HWM (High Water Mark).
**Code Rule:** Todo retorno debe ser Log-Returns ($ln(P_t / P_{t-1})$), nunca aritmético simple para series temporales.
